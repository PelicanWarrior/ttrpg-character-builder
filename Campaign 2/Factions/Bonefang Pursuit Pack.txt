Bonefang Pursuit Pack

Type:
Trandoshan hunting and slaver strike unit

Allegiance:
Historically contracted by Trandoshan hunt brokers, slaver syndicates, and crime middlemen operating between Kashyyyk routes and Outer Rim exchanges

Reputation:
Efficient, cruel, trophy-driven, and known for leaving settlements alive just long enough to spread fear

Pack Backstory:
The Bonefang Pursuit Pack began as a splinter cell of Trandoshan trophy hunters who were exiled from larger guild circles for keeping too much profit and disobeying extraction quotas. Rather than disband, they turned independent and built their own model: raid small settlements, capture labor-capable targets, take symbolic trophies, and sell captives through intermediary brokers instead of direct clan channels.

Over time, their methods became infamous. They used fear signatures (paint marks, ritual damage patterns, and selective survivors) to build a reputation that made later targets break faster. The Pack specialized in woodland and vertical-terrain pursuit, making them particularly dangerous around Wookiee villages and canopy routes.

Connection to Lowhhrick:
Lowhhrick remembers them as the raiding party responsible for the deaths of his sister Rhavarra and nephew Kettarr. He does not know their true names, only their identifying features.

Operational Doctrine:
- Strike at dusk or storm cover
- Disable lookouts first
- Burn escape paths only after captures are secured
- Keep one witness if intimidation value outweighs immediate kill value
- Prioritize captives with strength, technical skill, or social leverage

Current Status:
Fragmented but active. Individual members still appear as enforcers, escorts, and specialist trackers in the Outer Rim under short-term contracts.

Known Members (GM-facing true names):
1. Krassk Ironjaw (leader)
2. Vek'thar Pale-Eye (marksman/tracker)
3. Drok Hookarm (breacher)
4. Sszek Yellowmark (pursuit scout)
