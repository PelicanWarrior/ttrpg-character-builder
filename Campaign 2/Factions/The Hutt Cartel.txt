The Hutt Cartel

Type:
Confederated Hutt criminal power bloc

Current campaign posture:
Entrenched underworld force in active competition with Black Sun influence.

Role in campaign:
- Patron, rival, or unstable ally depending on player choices
- Control of routes, tribute systems, and enforcement assets
- Strong leverage in negotiations around logistics and movement

GM note:
Keep the Cartel as a political weight as much as a combat threat. Their influence should be felt through pressure, debt, and territorial control.
