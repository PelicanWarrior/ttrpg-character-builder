Rapa

Once a devoted member of the Order of the Shattered Light — a radical offshoot of the Jedi Order — Rapa dedicated himself to exposing the corruption festering within the parent Jedi Council and the galaxy at large. He believed the Shattered Light could be the beacon that would reform or replace the failing Jedi ways.
He fought tirelessly from within, challenging the hypocrisy, the political entanglements, and the growing blindness of both orders. But his warnings fell on deaf ears. The leaders of the Shattered Light proved just as rigid and self-righteous as the Jedi they had broken away from.
Disillusioned and exhausted, Rapa eventually stopped fighting their ignorance.
Then came the massacre on Tatooine.
In a single merciless operation, forces aligned with the Order of the Shattered Light slaughtered thousands of innocent civilians in Mos Espa, believing that a future evil would one day rise from that very place. The sheer scale of the atrocity shattered whatever faith Rapa had left.
He walked away that day, renouncing both the Jedi and the Order of the Shattered Light.
He is neither Jedi nor Sith. He follows no banner but his own — a true Grey Jedi, neutral and unbound.
For years he wandered the Outer Rim, honing his combat skills and mastering fear as a weapon. His black lightsaber and aura of dread became the stuff of legend.
Now, three years after the destruction of Mos Espa, word has reached Rapa of a Sith holocron hidden inside the wreckage of an ancient crash site on a barren moon orbiting Dathomir. The rumors speak of an Old Republic relic — a droid carrying forbidden Sith knowledge that could tip the balance of the galaxy.
Drawn by the danger and the chance to keep such power out of the wrong hands, Rapa sets course for the moon. He has no intention of claiming the holocron for himself… only of ensuring it never falls into the hands of those who would repeat the horrors of Mos Espa.
