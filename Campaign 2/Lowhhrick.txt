Lowhhrick

LOWHHRICK'S story

The Wookiees of the heavily forested planet Kashyyyk are a proud species
with a rich culture and sophisticated technology. Unfortunately, their bestial
appearance and inability to speak Basic often leads to the misconception that
all Wookiees are little more than savage brutes, A long-standing antagonism
between the Wookiees of Kashyyyk and the lizard-like Trandoshans frequently
flares up as Trandoshans hunt Wookiees for sport. Lowhhrick's sister and
nephew were victims of one of these Trandoshan raiding parties, and both
were killed. Lowhhrick vowed to hunt down the Trandoshans responsible...and
was promptly captured . So began his long career as a gladiator, forced to do
battle for the amusement of others

The raiders responsible were known among local hunters as the Bonefang
Pursuit Pack, a Trandoshan slaving-and-sport hunting party feared for taking
trophies from every settlement they struck. Lowhhrick never learned their true
names, but he never forgot what he saw that day: one towering Trandoshan with
an iron jaw brace riveted across his snout; one with a milky left eye and a long
burn scar from brow to throat; one broad-shouldered hunter whose right arm was
a crude mechanical replacement ending in hooked claws; and a lean tracker with
broken horn-spines and a bright yellow handprint painted across his chest armor.

Lowhhrick was raised in Vroshyr's Rest, a small Wookiee settlement built
around the roots of an ancient wroshyr tree far from the main trade routes.
His father, Grarruk, was a respected woodcraft hunter and trail guide, and his
mother, Shyyla, was a healer who maintained salves, splints, and old clan rites.
His older sister, Rhavarra, was bold and fiercely protective, and her young son,
Kettarr, followed Lowhhrick everywhere, eager to become a warrior like his
uncle. The Trandoshan raid that took Rhavarra and Kettarr was the wound that
shaped the rest of Lowhhrick's life.

Lowhhrick eventually achieved such fame that his contract was acquired by
a Hutt gangster named Zorba and he was brought to Ryloth. Zorba would
periodically send Lowhhrick into the ring to vanquish gladiators belonging
to his business "partners" as a means of establishing his dominance. From
time to time Lowhhrick was asked to serve as bodyguard during particularly
important "business meetings," where his presence would serve as a reminder
that Zorba had many trained killers on his payroll.

Over time, Lowhhrick earned a reputation not only as a fearsome gladiator but
as a disciplined enforcer who honored his word. After years of service, Zorba
offered him a brutal bargain: complete one high-risk mission on Tatooine, and
his freedom would be granted. Lowhhrick accepted, seeing it as his one chance
to break his chains and reclaim his life. The mission became the proving ground
that would decide whether he remained a weapon in Zorba's hands or finally won
the right to walk his own path.

Lowhhrick has a great deal of experience with combat, both from his career as
a gladiator and his time acting as a warrior on Kashyyyk. He is disciplined and
thoughtful by nature, but his experiences of the past few decades have made him
somewhat short-tempered. He takes his obligations and his oaths very seriously,
and nothing is more important to him than escaping his predicament and getting
vengeance for his family. Although Lowhhrick cannot speak Basic, he understands
it quite well and the other PCs understand his language just as well.
