SHADOWS OF THE OUTER RIM

It is a time of relative peace. The Core Worlds flourish under the watchful protection of the Jedi Knights and the Galactic Republic. But in the lawless Outer Rim, chaos reigns as rival criminal empires clash for dominance.

Three years ago, the bustling spaceport of Mos Espa on Tatooine was suddenly and mysteriously destroyed by an unknown organization. Crime lord Jabba the Hutt, convinced the attack was orchestrated by the ruthless syndicate known as Black Sun, has united the scattered Hutt clans and forged a powerful criminal army to strike back.

Now, after nearly three years of brutal warfare that has torn the Outer Rim apart, the galaxy holds its breath.

Campaign Use Notes:
- This conflict is the political and criminal backdrop for Campaign 2.
- Local missions may seem isolated, but most factions now answer to larger wartime pressures.
- Smuggling lanes, bounty markets, slave routes, and salvage rights are all destabilized by this war.
