Vroshyr's Rest, Kashyyyk

Type:
Small Wookiee settlement

Region:
Deep forest canopy routes, far from primary trade lanes

Overview:
Vroshyr's Rest is a compact Wookiee village built around a massive old wroshyr trunk, with rope-bridge walkways, elevated platforms, and hidden lower-root shelters. The settlement is designed for defense and survival over commerce, and outsiders rarely find it without a local guide.

Appearance:
- Layered wooden platforms anchored into wroshyr bark
- Rope lifts and suspended walkways between homes
- Covered rain catchments and root-fed freshwater channels
- Healing alcoves carved into inner trunk chambers
- Defensive alarm lines woven through upper canopy routes

Culture:
Vroshyr's Rest values oath-keeping, family protection, and practical self-reliance. Young Wookiees are trained early in climbing, tracking, and defensive combat, with strong emphasis on protecting noncombatants during raids.

Current status:
The settlement endured repeated Trandoshan slave-hunting pressure. Losses from one major raid, including members of Lowhhrick's family, continue to shape surviving clan memory.
