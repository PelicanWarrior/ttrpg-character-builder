The Luminous Hollows

Type:
Crystal cave network near Vael Tarris

Distance from city:
About half a day of travel from Vael Tarris along old pilgrim routes.

Overview:
The Hollows are deep cave chambers lined with pale-blue and silver crystal growths. They are used as listening and attunement spaces rather than formal shrine architecture.

Force behavior:
The crystals refract both sound and subtle resonance. Visitors often experience intuition spikes, memory flashes, and emotional amplification in deeper chambers.

Notable chamber:
Mirror Vault, where echo behavior is unnaturally out of sequence.

Campaign use:
- Pilgrimage trial location
- Controlled crystal retrieval arc
- Secret meeting or hidden research site
