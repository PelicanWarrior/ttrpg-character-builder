Jedha

Type:
Ancient pilgrimage world with strong Force traditions

Overview:
Jedha is revered by multiple Force-aligned traditions. Ruined shrines, old processional roads, and monastic enclaves mark it as a long-standing spiritual crossroads.

Campaign role:
Jedha can serve as a moral and spiritual counterweight to Mustafar's weapon-driven arc. It is suitable for guidance scenes, difficult ritual choices, and lore discovery.

Key child locations:
- Vael Tarris, The Pale City of Jedha
- The Luminous Hollows
