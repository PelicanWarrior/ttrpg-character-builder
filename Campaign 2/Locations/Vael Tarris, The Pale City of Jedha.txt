Vael Tarris, The Pale City of Jedha

Type:
Sacred canyon city carved into pale stone

Overview:
Vael Tarris is carved directly into white limestone escarpments. Prayer towers, cliff-side halls, and linked terraces define the skyline. The city is built inside the rock, not on top of it.

Force ties:
Kyber-bearing stone veins beneath the city create subtle resonance effects. Force-sensitive visitors often report heightened clarity, memory recall, and visions during ritual practice.

Atmosphere:
Wind chimes, tower bells, banners, and continuous prayer activity make the city feel alive with controlled spiritual intensity.

Campaign use:
- Ritual and guidance scenes
- Multi-order political and philosophical tension
- Archive and testimony retrieval
