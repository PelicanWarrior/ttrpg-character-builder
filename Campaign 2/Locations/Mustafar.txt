Mustafar

Type:
Volcanic Outer Rim world and dark-side nexus

Campaign role:
Primary destination for the Resonance Foundry arc.

Overview:
Mustafar is an ancient volcanic world marked by magma rivers, ash storms, and unstable terrain. It is deeply tied to dark-side history and old Sith industrial activity. The party's leads indicate this is the location of the hidden kyber-testing complex linked to the Ashen Testament thread.

Current relevance:
- Coordinates and historical clues point to a buried Sith research site.
- The Foundry is tied to weapon experiments using stressed kyber crystals.
- This world is likely to escalate Force-related character conflict.
