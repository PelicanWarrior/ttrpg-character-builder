The Resonance Foundry

Type:
Buried Sith kyber research and testing complex on Mustafar

Access:
Reached from a hidden landing platform with a broken gangway route over lava flow.

Core concept:
The Foundry used normal, intact kyber crystals and pushed them into fracture states through deliberate stress-testing to unlock new weapon behaviors.

Known structure:
- Factory Entrance (lava crossing and breach hazards)
- Level 1: The Stripped Upper Works
- Level 2: The Sealed Harmonic Vault

Level 1 summary:
Mostly ruined industrial shell with no meaningful loot. Atmosphere and traversal pressure.

Level 2 summary:
Main testing zone, living quarters, and research office with salvageable reports and prototype records.

Key narrative value:
- Introduces cracked-kyber prototype weapons and their risks.
- Confirms the flaw may be reduced through enough time, resources, and disciplined research.
- Provides a long-form upgrade path instead of a dead-end curse mechanic.
