Campaign 2 Folder Guide

Use this folder split to keep player-facing material separate from GM-only material.

Player-safe files:
- Root character backstory files such as Tol.txt
- Player Notes\
- Locations\
- NPCs\
- Factions\

GM-only files:
- GM Notes\
- Session Summaries\
