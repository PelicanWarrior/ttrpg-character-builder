Tol - Future Hooks

Hook 1: Survivor rumor
A freed laborer or captured slaver mentions an older Twi'lek healer matching Trethi's description. This should feel uncertain at first, not definitive.

Hook 2: The lantern token
Tol finds a carved beacon shard or family token from Nabat's Lantern in a place it should never be. This confirms that someone from the enclave survived long enough to carry or sell it.

Hook 3: The Iron Vulture return
Droven Kalt resurfaces as security for a crime lord, warlord, or off-book Imperial client. He remembers Tol and resents the moral line Tol drew.

Hook 4: Zygerrian records
A slaver ledger, prisoner manifest, or seized datapad lists captives from the Ryloth raid. This can give names, false hope, and painful clarity all at once.

Hook 5: Verashi's map
A hidden canyon map left by a survivor leads to a sealed cache from Nabat's Lantern containing names, history, and Bilmil's final message.

Hook 6: Trethi's legacy
Even if Trethi is dead by the time Tol reaches her trail, she may have left behind written remedies, stories, or testimony that restores part of what was lost.

Hook 7: Mercy test
Tol is put in a situation where sparing a defeated enemy may protect innocents later, echoing Trethi's lessons and testing whether he truly escaped the Iron Vulture mindset.
