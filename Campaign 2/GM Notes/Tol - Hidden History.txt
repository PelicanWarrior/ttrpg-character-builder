Tol - Hidden History

GM-only summary:
Tol does not know the full fate of Nabat's Lantern. The raid did not simply wipe the enclave out. It scattered it.

Suggested truth:
- Bilmil died holding the western gully approach long enough for noncombatants to escape through the Hollow Vault tunnels.
- Trethi survived the initial raid and was taken offworld with several other captives because the Zygerrians recognized her value as a healer who could keep enslaved laborers alive.
- A small pocket of Nabat's Lantern survivors escaped deeper into the canyon system under the guidance of an elderly scout named Verashi.
- One survivor, Nalen Vri, later sold information about other refugees in exchange for his own life. This betrayal is not yet known to Tol.

Who is still alive, if you want ongoing hooks:
- Trethi: alive, older, exhausted, still helping others survive in captivity or after escape
- Verashi: alive, hidden on Ryloth, protecting records, heirlooms, and names of the lost
- Nalen Vri: alive, compromised, ashamed, and vulnerable to blackmail

Who ordered the raid:
The local operation was financed through a Zygerrian trade broker named Daro Vexis, who subcontracted the actual violence to regional slavers and armed hirelings. This keeps the enemy chain broad enough for future investigation.

Best reveal structure:
1. First reveal that some survivors existed at all
2. Then reveal that Trethi may still be alive or may have been alive after the raid
3. Only later reveal the betrayal that allowed later captures
4. Save Bilmil's last stand for a powerful emotional confirmation scene, witness, or recovered account
