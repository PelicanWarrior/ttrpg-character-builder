1) The Bent Coil
Type:
Ship parts and repair components

What it sells:
- Hyperdrive couplings (mostly refurbished)
- Power relays, shield emitters, and control boards
- Hull patch kits and pressure seals

A brilliant mechanic with zero patience for fools. Patch can source almost anything mechanical if paid up front or owed a major favor.
