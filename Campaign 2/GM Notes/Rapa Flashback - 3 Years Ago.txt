Rapa Flashback - 3 Years Ago

Purpose:
- Cold-open scene for next session.
- Establish Rapa's prior connection to Force relic culture and why present-day choices matter.

Scene goals:
- Show Rapa before the current campaign pressure.
- Plant an emotional or philosophical fault line that can resurface on Jedha.
- Link the flashback to present relic decisions without revealing every answer.

Suggested structure:
1) Opening image:
- Rapa in a sacred or disputed site, receiving a warning about relic hunger and corruption.

2) Choice moment:
- Rapa either takes, hides, or refuses a Force-touched object.
- A mentor, rival, or witness reacts strongly.

3) Cost:
- Immediate consequence: lost trust, injury, exile, or a broken oath.

4) Bridge to present:
- End on a line or symbol that echoes in current events.

GM outcomes to pick before play:
- What truth from the flashback is public to the party?
- What truth stays private for later reveal?
- Which NPC from the flashback can return in the main timeline?
