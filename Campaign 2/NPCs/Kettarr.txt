Kettarr

Role:
Lowhhrick's nephew

Species:
Wookiee

Home:
Vroshyr's Rest, Kashyyyk

Summary:
Kettarr was Rhavarra's son, curious and energetic, and deeply attached to his uncle Lowhhrick. He followed Lowhhrick through training paths whenever allowed and wanted to become a guardian of the village. He was killed in the same Trandoshan raid as his mother.

Personality:
Curious, brave, warm-hearted, eager to learn

Story use:
Kettarr represents Lowhhrick's lost future and the innocence destroyed by slave raids.
