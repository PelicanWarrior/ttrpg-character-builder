Bilmil

Role:
Tol's father

Species:
Twi'lek

Home:
Nabat's Lantern, Ryloth

Public history:
Bilmil was one of Nabat's Lantern's chief watchmen and most reliable hunters. Quiet, disciplined, and slow to anger, he was known for noticing danger before anyone else did. He preferred action over speeches and taught through repetition, routine, and example.

What Tol learned from him:
- Read terrain before reading people
- Patience keeps you alive longer than pride
- A weapon should come out late, not early
- If you keep watch for others, they can sleep without fear

Personality:
Reserved, practical, steady, protective

Details Tol would remember:
- He checked straps, knots, and weapons twice
- He marked safe canyon routes with almost invisible stone scratches
- He could stand still for so long that children joked he was part of the cliff
- He rarely praised directly, but a nod from him meant everything

Story use:
Bilmil is a strong moral touchstone for Tol. Even if dead, missing, or changed by hardship, he should represent discipline without cruelty.
