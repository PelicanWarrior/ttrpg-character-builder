Trethi

Role:
Tol's mother

Species:
Twi'lek

Home:
Nabat's Lantern, Ryloth

Public history:
Trethi served as healer, mediator, and keeper of stories in Nabat's Lantern. She knew herbs, poultices, splints, and the old songs that helped frightened children sleep through storms. She had a gift for making people feel seen, even in grief.

What Tol learned from her:
- Strength without mercy becomes tyranny
- A community survives by remembering each other
- Healing is as brave as fighting
- Stories carry people when food and hope run thin

Personality:
Warm, perceptive, resilient, quietly stubborn

Details Tol would remember:
- The smell of dried herbs and heated stone in her shelter
- Her habit of touching a person's shoulder before speaking hard truths
- The way she turned history into lessons instead of nostalgia
- Her refusal to let fear make the enclave cruel

Story use:
Trethi is the emotional heart of Tol's past. Her fate is a strong lever for later reveals, hope, grief, or moral conflict.
