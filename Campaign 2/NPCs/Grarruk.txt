Grarruk

Role:
Lowhhrick's father

Species:
Wookiee

Home:
Vroshyr's Rest, Kashyyyk

Summary:
Grarruk is a veteran woodcraft hunter and route guide known for patience, silent movement, and strict survival discipline. He taught Lowhhrick how to track by scent shifts and broken canopy lines, and how to conserve strength in prolonged fights.

Personality:
Steady, practical, protective, stern but fair

Story use:
Use Grarruk as the source of Lowhhrick's discipline and tactical restraint. He can appear as a living elder, a recovered memory, or a remembered voice of duty.
