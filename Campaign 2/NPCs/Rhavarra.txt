Rhavarra

Role:
Lowhhrick's older sister

Species:
Wookiee

Home:
Vroshyr's Rest, Kashyyyk

Summary:
Rhavarra was bold, fast, and fiercely protective of her family. She often volunteered for perimeter watches and escort runs through risky canopy lanes. She was killed during a Trandoshan raid.

Personality:
Fearless, loyal, decisive, protective

Story use:
Rhavarra's death is a core trigger for Lowhhrick's hatred of Trandoshan slavers and his refusal to abandon family oaths.
