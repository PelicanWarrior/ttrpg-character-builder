Shyyla

Role:
Lowhhrick's mother

Species:
Wookiee

Home:
Vroshyr's Rest, Kashyyyk

Summary:
Shyyla is a healer who maintained salves, stitching kits, and old clan rites in Vroshyr's Rest. She was known for calming frightened children during storm seasons and treating hunters returning from dangerous patrols.

Personality:
Compassionate, resolute, perceptive, quietly authoritative

Story use:
Use Shyyla as the emotional and moral center of Lowhhrick's past, especially in scenes about mercy, endurance, and what is worth protecting.
